01 Local minima和Saddle point

Tayler Series Approximatioon

损失函数$L(\theta)$在某个值$\theta = {\color{blue} \theta^{\prime}}$ 可以用泰勒级数近似:
$$
L(\boldsymbol{\theta}) \approx L\left(\boldsymbol{\color{blue} {\theta}^{\prime}}\right)+\left(\boldsymbol{\theta}-\boldsymbol{\color{blue} {\theta}^{\prime}}\right)^{T} {\color{green}{\mathbf g}}+\frac{1}{2}\left(\boldsymbol{\theta}-\boldsymbol{\color{blue} {\theta}^{\prime}}\right)^{T}{\color{red} H}\left(\boldsymbol{\theta}-\boldsymbol{\color{blue} {\theta}^{\prime}}\right)
$$

其中, Gradient ${\color{green}{\mathbf g}}$ 是一个 vector :

$$
{\color{green}{\mathbf g}}=\nabla L\left(\boldsymbol{\theta}^{\prime}\right) \quad {\color{green}{\mathbf g}}_{i} = \frac {\partial L\left(\boldsymbol{\theta}^{\prime}\right)}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}_{i}}
$$

Hessian ${\color{red} H}$ 是一个 Hessian Matrix[1]

$$
{\color{red} H}_{i j}=\frac{\partial^{2}}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}_{i} \partial \boldsymbol{\theta}_{j}} L\left(\boldsymbol{\color{blue} {\theta}^{\prime}}\right)
$$

简记 $v=(\theta - {\color{blue} {\theta}^{\prime}})$, 判断$v^T{\color{red} H}v$的正定性

  • For all $v$
    $$
    \begin{gather}
    {\text{ if } \quad } \boldsymbol{v}^{T} {\color{red} H} \boldsymbol{v}>0 \Rightarrow \text { Around } \boldsymbol{\color{blue} \theta^{\prime}}: L(\boldsymbol{\theta})>L\left(\boldsymbol{\color{blue} \theta^{\prime}}\right) \Rightarrow \underline{\text { Local minima}} \nonumber \\
    {\color{red} H} {\text { is positive definite, all eigen values are positive}} \nonumber
    \end{gather}
    $$

  • For all $v$
    $$
    \begin{gather}
    {\text{ if } \quad } \boldsymbol{v}^{T} {\color{red} H} \boldsymbol{v}>0 \Rightarrow \text { Around } \boldsymbol{\color{blue} \theta^{\prime}}: L(\boldsymbol{\theta})>L\left(\boldsymbol{\color{blue} \theta^{\prime}}\right) \Rightarrow \underline{\text { Local minima}} \nonumber\\
    {\color{red} H} {\text { is positive definite, all eigen values are positive}} \nonumber
    \end{gather}
    $$

  • Sometimes
    $$
    \text { sometimes } \boldsymbol{v}^{T} {\color{red} H} \boldsymbol{v}>0, \text { sometimes } \boldsymbol{v}^{T} {\color{red} H} \boldsymbol{v}<0 \Rightarrow \underline {\text { Saddle point }}
    $$

在鞍点处,可以通过Hessian ${\color{red} H}$ 找到参数继续更新的方向 ${\color{red} H}$ may tell us parameter update direction

$\boldsymbol{u}$是${\color{red} H}$的一个特征向量，$\lambda$是特征向量$\boldsymbol{u}$对应的特征值，$\boldsymbol{u}^{T} {\color{red} H} \boldsymbol{u}=\boldsymbol{u}^{T}(\lambda \boldsymbol{u})=\lambda|\boldsymbol{u}|^{2}$
那么当$\lambda<0$时，$\lambda|\boldsymbol{u}|^{2}<0 \Rightarrow \boldsymbol{u}^{T} {\color{red} H} \boldsymbol{u}<0$
那么$L(\boldsymbol{\theta}) \approx L\left(\boldsymbol{\color{blue} {\theta}^{\prime}}\right)+\frac{1}{2}\left(\boldsymbol{\theta}-\boldsymbol{\color{blue} {\theta}^{\prime}}\right)^{T} {\color{red} H}\left(\boldsymbol{\theta}-\boldsymbol{\color{blue} {\theta}^{\prime}}\right) \Rightarrow L(\boldsymbol{\theta})<L\left(\boldsymbol{\color{blue} {\theta}^{\prime}}\right)$

这就说明在$\boldsymbol{u}=\theta - {\color{blue} {\theta}^{\prime}},\theta={\color{blue} {\theta}^{\prime}}+\boldsymbol{u}$情况时，此时$ {\color{blue} {\theta}^{\prime}}$可以往与某个负的特征值的特征向量$\boldsymbol{u}$相加的方向继续更喜，可以得到更小的$L(\boldsymbol{\theta})$。(this method is seldom used in practice)

02 Batch和Momentum

Small Batch vs Large Batch

  1. Larger batch size does not require longer time to compute gradient unless batch size is too large
  2. Smaller batch requires longer time for one epoch (longer time for seeing all data once)
  3. Optimization fails withwith large batch size，smaller batch size has better performance
    • Smaller batch size- Noisy update is better for training $\leftarrow$ small batch accompany with alterance for loss function $L = {L_1,L_2,…,L_N}$
    • Small batch is better on testing data[2]

Momentum

Movement not just based on gradient, but previous movement.
Movement: movement of last step minus gradient at present

03 自适应调整学习率

Adagrad

Training stuck because the parameters are around a critical point … and something else

梯度下降参数更新
$$
\begin{aligned}
\boldsymbol{\theta}_{i}^{t+1} & \leftarrow \boldsymbol{\theta}_{i}^{t}-\eta \boldsymbol{g}_{i}^{t} \quad \boldsymbol{g}_{i}^{\boldsymbol{t}}=\left.\frac{\partial L}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}_{i}}\right|_{\boldsymbol{\theta}=\boldsymbol{\theta^t}} \\
\boldsymbol{\theta}_{i}^{t+1} & \leftarrow \boldsymbol{\theta}_{i}^{t}- {\color{red} \frac{\eta}{\sigma_{i}^{t}}} \boldsymbol{g}_{i}^{t}
\end{aligned}
$$

$ {\color{red} \sigma_i^t}$ is parameter-dependent and iteration-dependent $\rightarrow$ 历史与当前梯度$g_i$的均方根Root Mean Square，Adagrad
$$
\begin{aligned}
\theta_i^{1} \leftarrow \theta_i^{0}-\frac{\eta^{0}}{\sigma_i^{0}} {\color{blue} g_i^{0}}\quad&\quad \sigma_i^{0}=\sqrt{\left({\color{blue} g_i^{0}}\right)^{2}}= \left | {\color{blue} g_i^{0}} \right | \\
\theta_i^{2} \leftarrow \theta_i^{1}-\frac{\eta^{1}}{\sigma_i^{1}} {\color{green}g_i^{1}} \quad&\quad \sigma_i^{1}=\sqrt{\frac{1}{2}\left[\left({\color{blue} g_i^{0}}\right)^{2}+\left({\color{green}g_i^{1}} \right)^{2}\right]}\\
\theta_i^{3} \leftarrow \theta_i^{2}-\frac{\eta^{2}}{\sigma_i^{2}} {\color{orange}g_i^{2}} \quad &\quad\sigma_i^{2}=\sqrt{\frac{1}{3}\left[\left({\color{blue} g_i^{0}}\right)^{2}+\left({\color{green}g_i^{1}} \right)^{2}+\left({\color{orange}g_i^{2}}\right)^{2}\right]} \\
\vdots \quad&\quad \vdots \\
\theta_i^{t+1} \leftarrow \theta_i^{t}-\frac{\eta^{t}}{\sigma_i^{t}} g_i^{t} \quad&\quad \sigma_i^{t}=\sqrt{\frac{1}{t+1} \sum_{i=0}^{t}\left(g_i^{t}\right)^{2}}
\end{aligned}
$$

RMSProp(Learning rate adapts dynamically)

梯度下降参数更新
$$
\boldsymbol{\theta}_{i}^{t+1} \leftarrow \boldsymbol{\theta}_{i}^{t}- {\color{red} \frac{\eta}{\sigma_{i}^{t}}} \boldsymbol{g}_{i}^{t}
$$
$ {\color{red} \sigma_i^t}$ is parameter-dependent and iteration-dependent $\rightarrow$ 历史$\sigma_i$与当前梯度$g_i$均方根Root Mean Square，RMSProp
$$
\begin{aligned}
\theta_i^{1} \leftarrow \theta_i^{0}-\frac{\eta^{0}}{\sigma_i^{0}} {\color{blue} g_i^{0}}\quad&\quad {\color{purple}\sigma_i^{0}}=\sqrt{\left({\color{blue} g_i^{0}}\right)^{2}}= \left | {\color{blue} g_i^{0}} \right | \\
\theta_i^{2} \leftarrow \theta_i^{1}-\frac{\eta^{1}}{\sigma_i^{1}} {\color{green}g_i^{1}} \quad&\quad{\color{red}\sigma_i^{1}}=\sqrt{\alpha\left({\color{purple}\sigma_i^{0}}\right)^{2}+(1-\alpha)\left({\color{green}g_i^{1}} \right)^{2}}\\
\theta_i^{3} \leftarrow \theta_i^{2}-\frac{\eta^{2}}{\sigma_i^{2}} {\color{orange}g_i^{2}} \quad &\quad \sigma_{i}^{2}=\sqrt{\alpha\left({\color{red}\sigma_i^{1}}\right)^{2}+(1-\alpha)\left({\color{orange}g_i^{2}} \right)^{2}}\\
\vdots \quad&\quad \vdots \\
\theta_i^{t+1} \leftarrow \theta_i^{t}-\frac{\eta^{t}}{\sigma_i^{t}} g_i^{t} \quad&\quad \sigma_{i}^{t}=\sqrt{\alpha\left(\sigma_{i}^{t-1}\right)^{2}+(1-\alpha)\left(\boldsymbol{g}_{i}^{t}\right)^{2}}
\quad (0<\alpha<1)
\end{aligned}
$$
$\sigma_{i}^{t-1}$是代表past gradients—— $[\boldsymbol{g_i^1},\boldsymbol{g_i^2},…,\boldsymbol{g_i^{t-1}}]$，因为$t-1$项，每一项都加权了一个比较小的$\alpha$，所以过去的梯度这部分对learning rate或者说对参数更新步长的影响较小；相反$\boldsymbol{g_i^{t}}$代表recent gradient,是自适应更新学习率的主要影响因素部分。

Adam:RMSProp + Momentum[3]

Learning Rate Scheduling

梯度下降参数更新
$$
\boldsymbol{\theta}_{i}^{t+1} \leftarrow \boldsymbol{\theta}_{i}^{t}- {\color{red} \frac{\eta^t}{\sigma_{i}^{t}}} \boldsymbol{g}_{i}^{t}
$$
$ {\color{red} \sigma_i^t}$ is parameter-dependent and iteration-dependent
$ {\color{red} \eta^t}$: learning rate varies with time(iteration-dependent)


Learning Rate Decay
As the training goes, we are closer to the destination, so we reduce the learning rate.

4 分类Classification

分类vs回归

回归问题

分类问题

Softmax

$$
y_i \rightarrow y_i^{\prime} \quad y_{i}^{\prime}=\frac{\exp \left(y_{i}\right)}{\sum_{j} \exp \left(y_{i}\right)} \quad 0<y^{\prime}<1 \quad \sum_i y_i^{\prime}=1
$$

Loss of Classification

  • 均方差Mean Square Error
    $$
    e=\sum_{i}\left(\widehat{\boldsymbol{y}}_{i}-\boldsymbol{y}_{i}^{\prime}\right)^{2}
    $$

  • 交叉熵Cross Entropy
    $$
    e=-\sum_{i} \widehat{y}_{i} \ln y_{i}^{\prime}
    $$

    Minimizing cross-entropy is equivalent to maximizing likelihood.

    损失函数

$$
L=\frac{1}{N} \sum_{n} e_{n}
$$

05 Batch Normalization

比如有个函数：$y = b+w_1x_1+w_2x_2$,两个输入的分布的范围很不一样，建议把他们的范围缩放，使得不同输入的范围是一样的。

Make different features have the same scaling

特征缩放的方法有很多：例如

  • Min-Max Scaling，就是将数据压缩至0~1之间，是当有新数据加入时, 可能导致和 发生变化， 所以这种算法对数据非常敏感；

  • z值归一化：将数值缩放到0附近，且数据的分布变为均值为0，标准差为1的标准正态分布等方法。

    For each dimension $i$:

         mean: $m\_i$
     standard variance: $\sigma\_i$​

    $$
    x_{i}^{r} \leftarrow \frac{x_{i}^{r}-m_{i}}{\sigma_{i}}
    $$
