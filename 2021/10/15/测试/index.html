对均值$\mu$参数估计Estimator of mean $\mu$
采样N个值${x^1, x^2,…, x^N} $,$m=\frac{1}{N} \sum_{n} x^{n} \neq \mu$

$$
E[m]=E\left[\frac{1}{N} \sum_{n} x^{n}\right]=\frac{1}{N} \sum_{n} E\left[x^{n}\right]=\mu
$$

$$
\operatorname{Var}[m]=\frac{\sigma^{2}}{N}
$$
